FI talk

Future Intelligence (FI) Talks is an online talk series occur every other Tuesday designed to spotlight the work of excellent early career researchers. Our aim is to enhance the visibility of these researchers and disseminate state-of-the-art research work in future intelligence to enthusiasts.
